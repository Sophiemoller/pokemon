PROJECT TITLE: PokeMon Card List
PURPOSE OF PROJECT: Store users Pokemon card collection digitally, 
allowing them to add new cards and their details, (Name, monetary value, and image)
also allowing users to find their cards by searching up the name and 
presenting its matching details. Hide card details at press of a button and image,
and display all cards in collection and their details.
